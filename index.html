Whats this DATA thingy all about?
DATA is a copenhagen based collaboration of 5 musicians, 1 VJ and 2 computer programmers who is trying to expand the limits of a human / technological interface in a live concert. We are building our own controllers, creating our own computer software and exploring new possibilites of playing music. We are not using any prerecorded material and all sounds / video pieces are triggered live.
DATA's awesome band members.
Jeppe Tuxen: Keys, Electronics Mads Andersen: Electric drums Rune Kaagaard: Keys, Joysticks Alice Carreri: Vocal, Vocoder, Autotune Jon Meinild: Samples, Wiimotes Turspin Djurhus: VJ and light
